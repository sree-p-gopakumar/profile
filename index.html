Passion Projects

SenseiWealth

In Development

Disciplined and structured portfolio management.

A personal-finance and investing-discipline app that tracks portfolios across the US, India, and Singapore — and enforces investing discipline through a personal rules vault, a vector-searchable mistake log, and AI-powered checks before any new investment is acted on. Built on the principle that good investing is about reflection over reaction.

Tech Stack
ReactTypeScriptTailwind Node.jsExpressPostgreSQL Chroma · RAGClaude APIClaude Vision DockerPWAFinnhub API
Modules
📊
Portfolio Dashboard

Unified multi-currency view (USD / INR / SGD) with live US equity pricing via Finnhub, allocation breakdowns, and an "investable worth" view that excludes illiquid assets.

📈
Net Worth Tracker

Monthly snapshots of the full financial picture — P&L by geography, FX-rate captures at snapshot time, and 12-month trend charts in INR and USD.

💰
Monthly Budget

Income and categorised expense tracking with carry-forward billing, named one-off line items, and Claude-flagged spending anomalies.

📜
Investing Rules Vault

A personal investing constitution. Drag-orderable rules across Entry, Exit, Sizing, Mindset, and Research — that gate every new idea before it can be acted on.

🧠
Mistake Log RAG

Private journal of past errors, embedded into a Chroma vector store. New ideas are semantically matched against past mistakes — Sensei warns you if you're about to repeat one.

💡
Idea Scratchpad Vision

Capture-and-evaluate workflow for new ideas. Claude Vision extracts ticker, target, and thesis from research screenshots; ideas can't graduate to "Approved" without rules acknowledgement.

🥋
XP & Belt Progression

Gamification layer that rewards consistent discipline. White → Black belt progression, daily streak counter, and bonus XP events for healthy habits.

🔔
Rule Reminder

Weekly nudge to re-read your principles. Banner shown automatically when entering the Idea Scratchpad if rules haven't been reviewed in 7+ days.

Why I built it: most investment trackers are passive — they show you what you have. SenseiWealth is active — it reminds you of your own rules, warns you when you're about to repeat a past mistake, and makes good financial habits feel like a game worth playing.
